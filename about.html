Our Story

Why we build

Namal Constructions Private Limited was founded on a simple frustration: in construction, the design team and the build team too often point fingers at each other while the client pays for the gap.

So we built a company where both live under one roof. Our engineers sit beside our designers. Our estimates are itemised. Our clients get one contract, one project manager and one number to call — 98840 00646.

From our base in Kathirvedu, Chennai, we have delivered independent homes, commercial spaces, renovations and turnkey interiors across the city — each one snag-checked before a single key changes hands.
