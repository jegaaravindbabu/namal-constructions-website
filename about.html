Who We Are

Building trust since 2012

Namal Constructions Pvt Ltd is a premier construction company headquartered in Chennai, with a strong operational presence across Tamil Nadu's major cities — Chennai, Coimbatore, Salem and Madurai. Since 2012, we have grown from a passionate local builder into one of the state's most trusted names in construction.

What began as a small team of dedicated professionals in Chennai has evolved into a statewide operation. Our founder envisioned a construction company that would never compromise on quality — no matter the size of the project. That vision drives us today, with one contract, one project manager and one number to call — 98840 00646.

With over a decade of hands-on experience, our portfolio spans residential complexes, commercial spaces, large-scale infrastructure and bespoke interiors — hundreds of projects, thousands of satisfied clients, and a reputation that speaks through the structures we create.
